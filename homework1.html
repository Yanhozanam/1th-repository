Introduction

Bujuumbura is a vibrant lakeside city with lively markets,
warm people and stunning views of Lake Tanganyika.

Geography

Geographically, Buja Town lies on the northeastern shore of Lake Tanganyika, surrounded by hills and mountains.
It is located in a lowland area with a tropical climate and rivers like Rusizi flow nearby,
making the region rich in natural beauty and biodiversity.

Spoken languages: English, French, Swahili and Kirundi

Kirundi, the national language and the most spoken, nearly by everyone.
English is increasingly being used by the youth and in international settings.
Swahili is common in markets and trade, especially among merchants.

Religion

Christianity: The majority religion, with Roman Catholicism being the most widespread, followed by various Protestant denominations.
Islam: Practiced by a significant minority, especially in certain neighborhoods.
Traditional beliefs still present in some communities, often blended with other religions.
